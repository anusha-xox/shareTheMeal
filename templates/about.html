About Us

Our website serves as a platform connecting restaurants with excess food to NGOs, enabling the efficient distribution of food to those in need. By leveraging location, food type, and quantity criteria, restaurants and NGOs can connect and ensure timely assistance. With the integration of a predictive machine learning model, restaurants can make informed decisions to reduce food wastage. Together, we strive to minimize food waste and combat hunger, one meal at a time.

Our Mission.

Our mission is to create a sustainable and compassionate ecosystem that connects restaurants with excess food to NGOs, enabling efficient distribution to those in need. We are dedicated to reducing food waste, combating hunger, and fostering social impact by leveraging technology and collaborative partnerships. Together, we strive to make a positive difference in our communities, one meal at a time.
